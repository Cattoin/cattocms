Services

Scope of Services: Sampark Sathi Foundation connects individuals in underserved communities with NGOs and government institutions to address needs in health, education, human rights, skills and livelihood, and government welfare schemes.
Volunteer Involvement: Volunteers play a crucial role in our operations. They are categorized as Community Volunteers (Case Givers) and Case Facilitators.
Community Volunteers: Local residents who identify and report issues within their communities. They come from diverse backgrounds and age groups, ranging from 18 to 80 years old. Their primary role is to act as the voice of Sampark Sathi within their communities, spreading awareness about the problems we can help solve.
Case Facilitators: Include college students, housewives, and corporate employees who assist beneficiaries in accessing services. Their responsibilities include document compilation, communication with authorities, and ensuring beneficiaries receive the services provided.
Beneficiaries: Our beneficiaries are individuals who receive services facilitated by Sampark Sathi Foundation. They may also become Community Volunteers after their cases are resolved, leveraging their personal experiences to help others.
