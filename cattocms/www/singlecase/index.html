FAQs about Fund a Beneficiary

The "Fund a Beneficiary" program by Sampark Sathi Foundation enables donors to directly support underprivileged individuals in need of specific products or services. Verified by our dedicated volunteers (Sathis), each beneficiary's case is documented and shared on social media to raise funds. Donors can connect directly with beneficiaries through the assigned Sathi, ensuring transparency and trust. This program leverages both college and corporate volunteers to manage cases of varying financial needs, ensuring efficient and compassionate assistance.

We have a dedicated team to verify details of every beneficiary. Our case managers make sure the details are accurate.

At Sampark Sathi, our volunteers are called "Sathis," which means a friend who is there for you when you need them. A Sathi is more than just a helper; they are a trusted companion dedicated to supporting beneficiaries in every step of their journey
