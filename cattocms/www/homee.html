About Us

A platform for solving social problems

Sampark Sathi Foundation is the only aggregator in India that connects people living in the underserved communities to NGOs/government institutions that can solve their problems across health, education, human rights, skill and livelihood and government welfare schemes.

Our Vision

Our vision is to make millions of individuals aware of and better able to access the resources offered by NGOs and governmental institutions.

We envision communities where every individual feels empowered to seek the support they need, knowing that help is always within reach.

Our goal is to ensure that no one faces their challenges alone, providing trusted companions to guide and support them every step of the way.

We aim to create lasting change by fostering self-sufficiency and resilience within communities, turning short-term assistance into long-term improvement.
